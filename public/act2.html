Mark's Recipe for the Best Garlic Butter Chicken

This is a simple and delicious Garlic Butter Chicken.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Cooking Tools

  • Knife
  • Cutting board
  • Frying pan
  • Spatula or wooden spoon
  • Measuring spoons
  • Mixing bowl

Procedure

  1. Heat the cooking oil and butter in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the garlic and saute` until fragrant.
  3. Add the chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  4. Season with salt and black pepper.
  5. Continue cooking until the chicken is fully cooked.
  6. Serve hot with rice.
